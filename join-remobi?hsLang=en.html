This FinTech scale-up is looking for a Senior Back End Engineer to work on their Risk team (with some front end experience). They're looking to revolutionise the payments infrastructure within the trucking and logistics industry, by building Stripe for Transportation.

The company are Series C funded, backed by a plethora of investors (Y Combinator, XYZ, Bloomberg Beta, etc.), founders and CEOs of big tech companies like Google, Coinbase and Salesforce and players in the space such as Mastercard and Flexport. They were also named on Forbes' "Next Billion Dollar Start-Up", showing the huge potential they have!

Responsibilities
  • Develop the platform enabling the company to verify and underwrite new customers while identifying and preventing fraudulent activities and coordinated attacks.
  • Create and enhance machine learning models to evaluate customer creditworthiness, loss risk, and other risk-related factors.
  • Collaborate with Risk and Product teams, including analysts, business operations, and underwriters, to formulate strategies, vision, and goals for managing customer credit, transaction, and payment risk.
  • Deliver robust technical solutions quickly and iteratively.
  • Contribute to defining the technical roadmap for risk engineering.
  • Ensure the right balance between rapid progress and maintaining high quality standards.
Who you are
  • Possess 4+ years of experience building B2B or B2C SaaS products, preferably in FinTech or financial services, with a focus on risk assessment and fraud detection at scale.
  • 4+ years of experience with Ruby (back end)
  • 2+ years of experience with React (front end)
  • Have strong experience in applying machine learning and data transformation to achieve significant business impact.
  • Self-motivated and dedicated to delivering business value.
  • Enjoy considering systems, their life cycles, edge cases, interactions, and failure modes.
  • Exhibit a strong sense of ownership and enjoy working on projects from start to finish in a fast-paced, high-autonomy environment.
