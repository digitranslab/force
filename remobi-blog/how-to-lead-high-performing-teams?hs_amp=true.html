How to Lead High-Performing Remote Teams

Written by Daniel Palmer | Oct 25, 2024 1:28:14 PM

Daniel Palmer, Remobi's Head of Client Engagement, spoke with Henry Poydar, Steady's CTO, about his "Continuous Coordination" methodology, offering insights to help you lead your own remote team revolution.

Continuous Coordination is the practice of using open, structured communication loops, alongside a set of proven collaboration principles, to give everyone in a digital product or service organisation precisely the shared context they need, when they need it, to stay productive, aligned, and engaged.

You can discover more about Henry and Continuous Coordination on their website.

 

Topic's of discussion included: 

1. The Remote Work Revolution 

Discover how remote work is not just a passing trend but a transformative revolution. Learn key insights on building and scaling enterprise-grade remote teams across borders, and how Remobi empowers companies to tap into global talent pools for success.

2. Challenges of Remote Work 

Explore the key challenges of remote work, including maintaining productivity, aligning teams across time zones, and ensuring efficient communication. Discover strategies to overcome these hurdles and keep your remote teams thriving. 

3. Solutions for Seamless Coordination 

Learn the importance of real-time shared context for remote teams. Discover asynchronous loops, strategies to reduce meeting fatigue, and how continuous coordination fosters transparency, trust, and accountability within teams.

4. Aligning Leadership and Remote Teams 

Discover how leadership can maintain alignment and productivity in distributed teams by implementing Continuous Coordination’s 7 principles. Learn how Remobi’s talent solutions support this framework for efficient and scalable remote team management. 

5. Building a Successful Remote Team 

A step-by-step guide to building and managing a remote team—from selecting and onboarding talent to overseeing productivity. Gain insights on nearshore teams and fostering a global community for successful collaboration.
